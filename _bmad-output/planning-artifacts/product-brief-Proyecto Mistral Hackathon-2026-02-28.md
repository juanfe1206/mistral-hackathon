# Product Brief: Proyecto Mistral Hackathon

## Executive Summary

Proyecto Mistral Hackathon is a salon-focused AI lead management assistant designed for small business owners who cannot afford large teams to manage inbound demand. Today, leads arrive through WhatsApp and calls, but owners rely on fragmented tools like spreadsheets and notebooks, creating manual, error-prone workflows and delayed responses. These delays directly result in lost customers, reduced revenue, and reputational damage.

The proposed solution centralizes lead handling and uses AI to classify lead urgency and type, prioritize follow-up actions, and guide timely responses with minimal manual coordination. By reducing operational chaos and response delays, the product helps salon owners recover missed opportunities and run a more reliable, professional customer experience.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Small salon owners struggle to manage incoming leads efficiently because conversations and follow-ups are split across WhatsApp, calls, spreadsheets, and notebooks. This fragmented workflow creates delays, disorganization, and missed responses that cause customer loss.

### Problem Impact

When leads are not handled quickly and consistently, potential clients choose competitors. The business loses customers, loses revenue, and weakens its reputation due to perceived poor responsiveness and service reliability.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Current approaches are mostly manual (spreadsheets, notebooks, ad hoc tracking) and require constant effort to maintain. They do not centralize communications, do not intelligently prioritize high-risk or high-value leads, and break down under time pressure, which is common for lean salon teams.

### Proposed Solution

Build an AI assistant for salons that centralizes lead management and automatically identifies lead types and priority levels. The assistant supports owners by organizing follow-ups, surfacing urgent opportunities, and reducing operational errors so leads are handled quickly and consistently.

### Key Differentiators

- Salon-first workflow focused on real lead-management pain in small teams.
- AI-driven lead classification and prioritization instead of static manual tracking.
- Centralized operational view replacing fragmented spreadsheets/notebooks.
- Designed for fast response execution to reduce customer drop-off and improve conversion outcomes.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

The primary users are small salon owners managing teams of approximately 2-5 staff members.
A representative persona is **Carla**, owner of a growing neighborhood salon.

Carla receives inbound interest from Google campaigns, Instagram content, and word of mouth. Most leads are funneled directly to her and handled through WhatsApp and calls. She currently tracks activity in spreadsheets and notebooks, which creates fragmented workflows, manual overhead, and high risk of missed follow-up.

**Motivations and goals:**
- Convert more inbound interest into booked appointments.
- Maintain business reputation through fast, reliable responses.
- Regain operational control without hiring additional staff.

**Core pain points:**
- Lost money from delayed or missed lead follow-up.
- Lack of visibility and control over lead status.
- Difficulty organizing communications across channels under time pressure.

**Success vision for this user:**
- More recovered leads and higher booking conversion.
- Better star ratings due to faster and more consistent communication.
- Increased loyalty and returning customers from reliable service quality.

## Success Metrics

Success for this product is measured by whether salon owners recover more at-risk leads, respond faster to high-value conversations, and build stronger customer loyalty while maintaining control of daily operations.

### Business Objectives

- Short-term (0-3 months): prove operational value for small salons by reducing missed follow-ups, improving visible service quality, and demonstrating measurable recovery and retention gains.
- Long-term (12 months): establish a reliable lead operations layer for lean salon teams, reduce customer churn from delayed communication, and improve reputation through consistent customer experience.

### Key Performance Indicators

- Lead Recovery Rate (%): increase recovered at-risk leads from an estimated 20% baseline to 35% at 3 months and 50% at 12 months.
- Reputation KPI (Star Rating): improve average rating from 4.3 to 4.6 by month 3 and 4.7+ by month 12.
- Returning Customer KPI (%/month): increase from 30% baseline to 40% at 3 months and 50% at 12 months.
- VIP/High-Risk Response SLA: >=90% of VIP/high-risk leads receive a first response in under 5 minutes.
- Low-Priority Automation Coverage: >=70% of low-priority inbound messages are auto-responded by AI with owner override.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

- AI lead classification into VIP, high-risk, and low-priority segments with explainable reason tags.
- At-risk pulse alert with one-click recovery reply generation for urgent leads.
- Priority queue and SLA tracking to keep VIP/high-risk leads under 5-minute response time.
- Low-priority AI auto-response with human override controls.
- Basic performance dashboard showing recovery, returning-customer, and response-time KPIs.
- Optional for MVP: centralized inbox for WhatsApp and call-derived lead notes.

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Multi-channel native integrations beyond WhatsApp and basic call notes (email, Instagram DM, full telephony integration).
- Fully autonomous sending for VIP/high-risk conversations (these remain human-approved).
- Advanced CRM capabilities (pipeline builder, campaign automation, segmentation engine).
- Multi-location or franchise management and enterprise-grade role/permissions.
- Full analytics suite with custom reports, exports, and long historical benchmarking.

### MVP Success Criteria

- Core feature set (classification, alerts, recovery replies, SLA tracking, low-priority automation, basic dashboard) is functional end-to-end.
- Aha moment is consistently demonstrated: the product flags an at-risk lead and supports booking recovery.
- Early pilot usage shows measurable improvement against baseline for recovery and response-time metrics.
- Owners report improved day-to-day control with short, repeated check-ins rather than manual spreadsheet/notebook juggling.

### Future Vision

- Expand channel coverage to include Instagram DMs, email, and deeper telephony/CRM integrations.
- Introduce smarter autonomy policies with confidence gating and auditable safety controls.
- Add richer retention intelligence and predictive churn prevention workflows.
- Scale from single-salon operations to multi-location teams with advanced permissions and reporting.
